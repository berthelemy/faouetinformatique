I've worked with information technology since the 1980s. It's something that I find natural and relatively painless. But I know that many people find technology difficult. My job is to help you over that pain.

I've been a teacher (of children aged 5-18) and, for the past 20+ years worked in corporate learning and international development.

I'm based near Le Faouët on the border of Morbihan and Finistère, and close to the south-western edge of Côtes-d'Armor.

I'm the editor, photographer and technician behind the Nestled in Brittany website - a guide to some of my favourite places in our beautiful region.
